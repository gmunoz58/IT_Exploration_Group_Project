Project Overview

Business Problem

Our e-commerce client needed to understand customer purchasing behavior, identify high-value customer segments, and optimize marketing strategies to increase customer lifetime value and reduce churn rates.

Data Sources

  • Customer transaction database (2 years of data)
  • Website clickstream analytics
  • Customer demographic information
  • Product catalog and category data
  • Marketing campaign performance metrics

Analytical Approach

We employed a comprehensive data analytics methodology including exploratory data analysis, customer segmentation using clustering algorithms, predictive modeling for churn prediction, and cohort analysis for customer lifetime value assessment.
