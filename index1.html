IT Field Deep Dive: Data & Analytics
What the field is, careers, insights from 5 Business Analysts, and a quick interactive challenge.

1) What Is Data & Analytics?

Definition

Collecting, cleaning, and transforming data to discover patterns, answer questions, and support decisions with evidence.

Role in Organizations

  • Expose trends & opportunities for growth
  • Support strategy and day‑to‑day operations
  • Measure outcomes and drive accountability

Daily Activities

  • Gather & validate data from systems
  • Clean, join, and analyze datasets
  • Build dashboards & explain insights to stakeholders

2) Career Exploration

Entry‑Level

  • Data Analyst — reports, dashboards, stakeholder support
  • BI Analyst — visualization (Power BI/Tableau), KPIs

Mid‑Level

  • Data Engineer — pipelines, quality, scalable data
  • ML Engineer — predictive models & automation

Senior

  • Data Scientist — advanced stats/ML aligned to strategy
  • Business Analyst — requirements, process, decision support

Core Skills & Certifications

  • Skills: SQL, Python/R, Excel, Power BI/Tableau, communication
  • Certs: Google Data Analytics, AWS/Azure Data, Microsoft Power BI, ICCP
